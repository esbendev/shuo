About

This is some practice material I use for my Chinese studies, specifically as aids for The Ultimate Beginner’s Handbook (I) by Shuo Shuo Chinese.

Click the buttons to access quizzes for each day. Some days are greyed out because I didn't think there was anything I could add on top of what the course already offered, and if there are days missing it's probably because I haven't gotten that far in the course yet!

Having said that, if you find any errors, have any suggestions or feedback, please let me know!
